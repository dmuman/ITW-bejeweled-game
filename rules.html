About the game

Swap crystals to make three same-colored crystals in a row. Every move has to make a match of three or more crystals. Clear crystals to advance the bar at the bottom of the board for it to reach the other end and level up. Each level higher means you need more matches to advance. If there are no more possible moves left, the game ends.

The progress bar on the bottom recedes to the left continuously after starting the level situated in the center. As you must advance it all the way to the right to level up, make matches quickly. The bar recedes faster with each passing level. If the bar is empty, the game ends.
